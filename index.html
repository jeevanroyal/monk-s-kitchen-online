Monk's Kitchen caters to the discerning clientele looking for an international dining experience in Vijayawada. With great service and a refreshing ambience to add to a gourmet cuisine, culinary aficionados now have a new place to call home.

Making way for a hearty meal is Monks Kitchen in Vijayawada. Established in the year 2016, this place is synonymous with delicious food that can satiate all food cravings. It is home to some of the most appreciated cuisines.
